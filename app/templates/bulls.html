Welcome to Bulls Bible School

Bulls Bible School exists to create an opportunity for in-depth theological training for people in ministry. We aim at establishing students in their most holy faith, not just intellectually but with a keen focus on the spirit aspect of our faith, with the intent of equipping them in the supernatural as well.

Why study with us?

Competency-Based Learning

Our curriculum nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and real-world skills for lifelong spiritual success.

Exceptional Training Experience

Our comprehensive facilities provide a safe, comfortable, and nurturing environment where students thrive spiritually.

Christianity-Based Foundation

We instill strong biblical values to shape responsible, disciplined, and morally upright ministry leaders.
